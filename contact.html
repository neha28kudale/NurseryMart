Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our plant collection, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or sign in to complete your purchase.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for plants that arrive damaged or unhealthy. Please contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery for any issues.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 days) is available for select locations. Delivery times may vary based on your location.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within India. We're working on expanding our international shipping options in the future.

How can I become a seller?

Register as a seller on our platform, complete the verification process, and start listing your plants. Our team will guide you through the onboarding process.
